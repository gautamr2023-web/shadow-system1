About Confidence Sliders
Go to Tests → Confidence tab. You'll see all your JEE topics listed under Physics, Chemistry and Maths.

For each topic, drag the slider to show how confident you feel:
■ 0–39% — Not studied / very weak
■ 40–79% — Studied but needs revision
■ 80–100% — Strong, mostly done

The app uses these to show your weakest topics in the JEE Marks section so you know where to focus next.
